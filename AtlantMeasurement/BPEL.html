How To

A predicate can be set for each metric column (an operator followed by a number). The values for the metric that respect the given predicate are emphasized.

Operators for the predicate are among:

  • lower than (&lt;)
  • lower or equal than (&lt;=)
  • equal to (= or ==)
  • not equal to (!= or !)
  • greater or equal than (&gt;)
  • greater than (&gt;=)

Unit for percentage values are not necessary. If no operator is given, the operator lower or equal than (&lt;=) is used. If the predicate is incorrect, no value is emphasized.

Predicate samples:

  • &gt;1
  • =33.3
